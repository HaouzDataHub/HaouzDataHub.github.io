About Me

Hey everyone, Hamza Haouzmani here. I entered the world of data analysis through an experience dictated by circumstances. I was in the construction industry, managing my own company at various stages, but after the COVID-19 pandemic, everything changed. Being passionate about finance and business, I began exploring the world of trading by learning about market movements. I mastered many classical and fundamental analysis methods, worked with various indicators, and learned to monitor liquidity and news. I discovered that scientific methods differ in methodology and that data analysis has rules and principles for achieving real results and making informed decisions. I enrolled in a Coursera course by Google and completed it. Now I'm studying case studies to prepare. My specialties are data visualization and translating data into business recommendations using R, SQL, Tableau, Google Sheets and Excel.
